**InterviewIQ** is your personal AI interview coach. Whether you are preparing for your first job or looking to switch careers, InterviewIQ provides a realistic mock interview experience to help you build confidence, improve your answers, and land your dream job!

---

## 🌟 What Can InterviewIQ Do For You?

- **🎤 Voice-Based Practice:** Answer interview questions out loud, just like in a real interview. Our advanced speech recognition instantly captures your responses.
- **🤖 Intelligent Feedback:** Get real-time, constructive feedback on your answers from our expert AI coach.
- **📊 Performance Reports:** After every interview, receive a detailed scorecard highlighting your strengths and pinpointing areas for improvement.
- **📈 Track Your Growth:** View your complete interview history to see how your skills improve over time.

## 📖 How to Get Started

### 1. Create an Account
Head over to the **Sign Up / Login** page to create your secure account. This allows us to save your progress and keep your interview reports private.

### 2. Set Up Your Interview
Navigate to the **Dashboard** and select "Start New Interview". You'll be asked a few quick questions to tailor the interview to your specific needs (such as your target role and experience level).

### 3. Take the Mock Interview
- Make sure you are in a quiet room and your microphone is working.
- The AI will ask you a series of questions.
- Speak naturally! Our system will transcribe your speech instantly and analyze your answer.
- You can review your transcribed answer before submitting it to the AI for evaluation.

### 4. Review Your Report
Once you complete the interview, a detailed **Interview Report** will be generated. Take the time to read through the AI's suggestions on how you can improve your tone, clarity, and the content of your answers.

## 💡 Tips for Success
- **Be in a quiet environment:** Background noise can interfere with the speech-to-text accuracy.
- **Speak clearly and at a normal pace:** Don't rush your answers. Treat the AI just like a real human recruiter.
- **Use the STAR method:** For behavioral questions, remember to describe the **S**ituation, **T**ask, **A**ction, and **R**esult!

Good luck with your preparation! If you have any questions or need support, feel free to reach out to our team.
